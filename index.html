I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Engineering at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology advised by Dr. (Mrs.) Theresa-Samuelle Adjaidoo.

My research centers on cooperative intelligence among multi-agent systems. I focus on enhancing coordination and interaction among autonomous agents in complex environments. My work explores efficient methods for information transmission, fusion, and decision-making across key tasks in perception, prediction, and planning.
